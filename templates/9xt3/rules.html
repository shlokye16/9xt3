Rules

9x9 Tic-Tac-Toe is played on a big board made of 9 small 3x3 boards.

  • Win a small board to claim that square on the big board.
  • Win the big board to win the game.
  • On your first move you can play anywhere.
  • After that, your move sends your opponent to a specific small board, whichever square you played in determines which board they must play in next.
  • If the board you're sent to is already won or full, you may play in any open board.
  • If a small board fills up with no winner, it's a draw and neither player owns it.

The maker of the game plays as O and moves second. The joiner plays as X and moves first.

Games expire after 72 hours of inactivity. You'll receive a warning email at 48 hours.
