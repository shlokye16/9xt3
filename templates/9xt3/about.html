About

Over winter break, I built this. My best friend and I used to play 9x9 tic-tac-toe all the time , the kind of game you play for an hour and lose track of time. Then college happened, and now we're miles apart. Video calls are fine, but they don't really replace sitting across someone and playing a dumb game like nothing's changed.

So I made this. You make a game, get a 6-letter code, send it to someone you know. They join. No matchmaking, no strangers: just two close friends, playing across distance like they're back in the same room.

The maker plays as O and moves second. The joiner plays as X and goes first.

For how the 9x9 board works, see Rules.

Built for my best friend. For everyone else too.
